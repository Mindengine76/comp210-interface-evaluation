Discussion

The study, although strong as a needs-gathering activity, can not be said to be a success in terms of an effective interface evaluation. This was due to not only the poor condition of the interface (functionality and aesthethics), but also to the haste that the interview and surveys were delivered. The study also suffered from a lack of participants and unclear questions.


It is safe to say that the data produced is limited, as an evaluation of the usability of the interface. But it has been instrumental in informing the design and direction of the prototype.
